Cash-Out Car Refinancing

Use Your Car’s Equity to Access a Lump Sum with Cash-Out Refinancing.

How It Works

It works by paying off your current car loan, taking out a new loan for more than you owe, and receiving the difference in cash. You can use this money for bills, expenses, or to pay off high-interest debts like credit cards, helping you avoid extra interest, penalties, and potential debt collection issues.

Benefits

  • Access to quick cash:
    You can get money for emergencies etc. without taking another expensive loan.
  • Lower interest rate:
    Qualifying for a better rate can reduce your overall loan cost.
  • Flexible loan terms:
    Extend your loan to lower monthly payments if eligible.

How Much Cash Can You Get From Car Refinancing?

The cash you can get depends on your car’s value and eligibility.
For example, if your car is worth $30,000 and you still owe $18,000, you could refinance for $30,000 and receive $12,000 in cash. If eligible, you might also get a lower interest rate and extend your loan to reduce monthly payments.

Why Consider Car Refinancing?

The cost of car loans in Singapore can vary widely, making your loan choice almost as important as your car. Many buyers take high-interest loans at dealerships just to get their car immediately.
Market conditions and interest rates change over time. Your current loan may no longer be the most cost-effective solution for your financial needs.
Refinancing could help reduce your monthly repayments—either through a lower interest rate or by extending your loan term.
Refinance to save money, improve cash flow, and get a loan that better suits your current financial situation.
