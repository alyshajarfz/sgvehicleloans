About AutoMotoCredit

AutoMotoCredit is a certified automotive financing company based in Singapore, specializing in car and motorcycle loans. We are fully licensed and regulated, ensuring our clients receive safe, transparent, and professional financial services.

Founded over 12 years ago, we have helped more than 6,500 customers secure their dream vehicles while maintaining a 99% customer satisfaction rate.
